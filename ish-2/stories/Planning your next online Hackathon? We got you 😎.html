Campus Experts applications are OPEN 🚩  

  

We’re back! Campus Experts applications are OPEN 🟢. This is your chance to apply to the program and empower your college technical community, by learning the necessary skills with training, mentorship, and support from GitHub. 

You don’t know what Campus Experts are 🤔? Check it out ⤵️ 

They are student leaders distributed all around the world, building a diverse and inclusive space in their communities, leading conferences, hackathons, maintaining open source projects, and even more 🌎. Learn with them in this video!

🪜How to apply:

  • Get the GitHub Student Developer Pack (if you don’t have it yet);
  • Complete the application form and submit it (check the requirements on FAQs);
  • Submit a video resume (if you were approved in the step number 2, you will get an invitation);
  • Welcome to the program: hooray, you were approved in the step number 3;
  • Complete the training: you only become a Campus Expert after finishing the training and submitting your proposal. 

💡Why should you apply to be a Campus Expert?

  • You will get training on how to lead your community;
  • Grow your career with speaking opportunities at GitHub conferences;
  • Exclusive swag and support from GitHub to organize your events;
  • Connect with other amazing students from all around the world;

Don’t miss this big opportunity 💖. Applications are open from August 4 until the beginning of September.
